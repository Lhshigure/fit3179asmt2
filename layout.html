Covid 19 Worldwide Impact 2021

What is Covid 19?

A new coronavirus designated Covid 19 was first identified in Wuhan, the capital of China's Hubei province People developed pneumonia without a clear cause and for which existing vaccines or treatments were not effective. The virus has shown evidence of human-to-human transmission Transmission rate (rate of infection) appeared to escalate in mid-January 2020 As of 30 January 2020

How much does Covid 19 affect people?

As we have seen, the number of deaths of covid19 patients increased explosively from March to April, decreased slowly from May to July, and began to increase gradually from June to July.

About the lethality of Coivd 19?

As we can see, although Coivd 19 does not have a high lethality rate it still causes a large number of deaths globally due to its highly contagious nature, with the number of infected people increasing rapidly over a six-month period.
